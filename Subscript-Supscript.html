Subscripts And SuperScripts

Five Examples Of Chemical Formulas

The different types of chemical formulas include:molecular,empirical,structural and condensed structural formulas.

SubScripts Examples:

Salt (NaCl)

Sucrose (C12H22O11)

Vinegar or acetic acid (C2H3O2)

Baking soda or sodium bicarbonate (NaHCO3)

SuperScripts Examples:

The formula of Pythagoras theorem is (a2 + b2 = c2)

We can write Alexandar as Alexr for record-keeping

Class Fifth can be also written as (5th)
